
If you've decided to part ways with the Sprinterwave app, whether it's for a short break or a permanent goodbye, we’ve made the process of closing your account straightforward. Here's a complete step-by-step guide on how to do it.

---

## Step 1: Open the Sprinterwave App

Launch the **Sprinterwave** app from your device’s home screen.

![Open the app](https://paper-attachments.dropboxusercontent.com/s_9E95652207F995D1D1ABD2D1A3FDF459693FA232EDA5C06CAF2C91558C7BE877_1743970006667_IMG_2223.PNG)


---

## Step 2: Go to Your Profile

Tap the **profile avatar** at the top-left corner of the screen. This will open a sidebar—simply click on "Profile Info" to navigate to the profile page where you can proceed to close your account.

![](https://paper-attachments.dropboxusercontent.com/s_9E95652207F995D1D1ABD2D1A3FDF459693FA232EDA5C06CAF2C91558C7BE877_1743950187873_IMG_2219.PNG)


---

## Step 3: Select "Close Account"

Scroll down until you see the **"Close Account"** button. Tap on it.

![](https://paper-attachments.dropboxusercontent.com/s_9E95652207F995D1D1ABD2D1A3FDF459693FA232EDA5C06CAF2C91558C7BE877_1743970973022_IMG_2220.PNG)


---

## Step 4:  What Gets Deleted
 
You'll be shown a summary of the data that will be permanently deleted if you choose to close your account. Please note that this action is irreversible, and once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.


![](https://paper-attachments.dropboxusercontent.com/s_9E95652207F995D1D1ABD2D1A3FDF459693FA232EDA5C06CAF2C91558C7BE877_1743950187525_IMG_2221.PNG)


---

## Step 6: Confirm With Transaction Pin

For security purposes, you’ll be asked to enter your transaction PIN. Provide the PIN to proceed with confirmation.

![](https://paper-attachments.dropboxusercontent.com/s_9E95652207F995D1D1ABD2D1A3FDF459693FA232EDA5C06CAF2C91558C7BE877_1743949638421_IMG_2222.PNG)

---

## Step 7: Done!

Once confirmed, your Sprinterwave account will be permanently deleted immediately. You’ll receive a confirmation email, and all associated data will be erased in line with our privacy policy.


---

## Can I Reopen My Account?

If you change your mind before the deletion process is finalized, you can contact our support team via the [**Contact**](https://www.sprinterwave.com/contact) section in the app or email us at **support@sprinterwave.com**.

---

> ⚠️ **Note:** Once the account is permanently deleted, it cannot be recovered.

---

Need help with anything else? Sprinterwave Support is always here for you!
